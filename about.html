About Our College

Excellence in pharmaceutical education since 1995

Welcome to Doctor Pharmacy

Our Doctor Pharmacy focuses on pharmaceutical sciences, patient care, and scientific research. Students learn through lectures, laboratory work, and clinical training. We are committed to producing skilled pharmacists who make a difference in healthcare.

With state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty, we provide hands-on training that prepares students for real-world challenges in the pharmaceutical industry.

Study Areas & Programs

Pharmacology

Study of drug action and effects on living systems.

Pharmaceutics

Design and manufacture of pharmaceutical products.

Clinical Pharmacy

Patient-centered care and medication management.
